Meet the Missionaries!

Are you unfamiliar with the temples of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints? The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints has Missionaries in each location that a temple is located in that would be more than willing to answer your questions! In each location, there is a mission headquarters that can direct you to missionaries in each of the areas.

Rexburg, Idaho Temple

The Rexburg Idaho Temple is located within the Idaho Falls Mission. The Idaho Falls Mission Headquarters can be reached at 208-123-4567.

Accra, Ghana Temple

The Accra Ghana Temple is located within the Ghana Accra Mission. The Accra Ghana Mission Headquarters can be reached at 111-222-333.

Seoul, South Korea Temple

The Seoul, South Korea Temple is located within the Korea Seoul Mission. The Korea Seoul Mission Headquarters can be reached at 123-4444-5555.

Kiev, Ukraine Temple

The Kiev, Ukraine is located within the Ukraine Kiev Mission. The Ukraine Kiev Mission Headquarters can be reached at 1234-2903-4847.
