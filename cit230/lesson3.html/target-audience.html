Our Target Audience

Who We Want to Reach Out To

Our target audience is going to be outdoor enthusiasts. There are many different outdoor locations you can do in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, Idaho. The weather is of paramount importance while planning these fun activites in these locations.

Five Scenarios for this SpotOn Weather.com:

What kind of weather is going on right now in my vacation destination?
Will I be able to plan my vacation from the information from this website?
How far out will this website give weather predictions?
Will the weather in the website's listed cities affect my trip up in the mountains, lake, etc.?
Will this website be easy to read?
