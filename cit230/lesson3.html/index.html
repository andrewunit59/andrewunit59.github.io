OnSpot Weather

Our web page for current weather in various Eastern Idaho locations

Idaho is a state full of natural beauty. It comes as no suprise that we have outdoor enthusiasts from all over the world who come and visit this great state, and to see what she has to offer. OnSpot Weather.com is an option for these outdoor enthusiasts to learn about the current weather in various locations so that they can plan their day of fun out in nature, without having to worry about unfavorable weather conditions. The web page will have the following webpages: Home, Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, Storm Center, and the Photo Galley.

We feel it is is a priviledge to serve these outdoor enthusiasts!
