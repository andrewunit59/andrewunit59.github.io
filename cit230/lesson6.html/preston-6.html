About Preston

Preston Idaho is a small little town located in Franklin, County Idaho. It has a population of a little over 5,000 people. Preston Idaho is famous for its Festival of Lights, which occurs every year from the day after Thanksgiving until December 31. Despite the rural setting of Preston, there are many different hikes and other activities that you can do in the area!
